Classes are colourful, joyfull, ventilated room, with friendly enviroment , and Theme based curriculum.

The curriculum includes English, Hindi, Math, Enviromental Studies, Moral Values, Art and craft, General Knowledge , Computer Literacy Programme and co-curricular activities.

Students pursue their hobbies and interests in class room activities.

The following exposure are given to the students:

  • Morning Assembly

  • Painting, Sketching and Paper Folding
