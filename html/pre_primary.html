Pre-Primary Curriculum

The Child Centric Curriculum in Pre – Primary is in accordance with Play-way method with activity- based learning.

The curriculum is designed to cater the individual child’s potential and interests.

Activities like Painting, Singing, Dancing, Moral & Values, Sports, Story Telling, Karate and various Competitions and celebrations provide ample of opportunities to develop and showcase their creativity.

A variety of hand on activities promote first hand experiences, useful for learning. As Maria Montessori education recognizes that children learn in different ways, and accommodates all learning styles.

Following are the Activities Included in the Curriculum:

  • Morning Assembly
  • Swings, Climbers, Slides
  • Nature Walk
  • Computer Literacy Programme
  • Activity Room - Include all Games-Ball Pool, Puzzles, Role Play, Flash Card, Teaching Tools, Clay Zone , Projector, Story Books Etc
  • Art and Craft
  • Festivals, Special Programmes, Birthday Celebrations, Grand Parents’ day
  • Winter Carnival With Parents, Sports day and Annual day
  • Annual Exhibition; Art And Craft Exhibition created by Students, are Exhibited
  • Excursions, Outings and Picnics
  • Annual Concert (Nursery And Prep) Once In A Year- Ensures 100% participation
  • Sports Activities

Children learn by play way method.
