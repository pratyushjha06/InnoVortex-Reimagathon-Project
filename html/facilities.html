Facilities

"Providing State-of-the-Art Facilities to Enhance Learning and Growth."

Facilities

COMPUTER CENTRE

Computer center has been designed for students to access Information Technology, to sharpen their analytical skills and stretch their creative talents as they learn new concepts. Computer use exponentially increases options for creative growth and aesthetic expression.

Interactive software programs help kids of all ages develop their strong points and build up their weaker areas. The Computer system is devised to promote team spirit and a sense of belonging to the school.

The computer center contains computers with updated softwares, a skilled computer teacher to guide the students and projector with smart boards for innovative learning experience.

SMART CLASS

Classrooms are equipped with a state of the art Innovated Teaching System (ITS) comprising of a highly versatile interactive white board and a sophisticated projection system. Teachers access the digital content in their classrooms on an everyday basis, project it on the board during their classroom sessions and explain concepts with the help of animations, graphics, video, presentation etc. It is interactive learning which the students enjoy thoroughly.

The Computer system is devised to promote team spirit and a sense of belonging to the school.

LIBRARY

A well ventilated and spacious library has been set up for our students with latest learning materials, digital resources and silent zones. Our Library facilitates each student as well as teachers with access to essential resources for a smooth learning process.

Students are encouraged to cultivate good reading habits, work on their research skills and rational thinking. The library as an important center for learning. Main aim is to encourage curiosity, innovation and problem solving skills which will help them in different areas of their life.

LABORATORY

Latest modular laboratories have been built for subjects such as Biology, Physics and Chemistry. These labs have been built with an aim of providing quality education to our students and to enhance their hands-on learning experiences that help student develop problem solving and critical thinking skills.
