About Us

DUV International School, established in 2020, is one of the best and leading educational School in Noida. The school is known for its excellent infrastructure, faculty, innovative teaching methodology, excellent results and a plethora of activities that are conducted throughout the year, which provide a solid foundation for students students to become confident scholars and responsible citizens.
